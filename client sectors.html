CLIENT SECTORS

RMP Legal Compliance and Risk Advisory services clients across a diverse range of industries, providing tailored legal, governance, compliance, and SHEQ solutions. Our expertise spans the following sectors:

  • Financial Services - Investment firms, co-operatives, micro-lenders, and other financial institutions requiring regulatory compliance and risk management support.
  • Legal and Professional Services - Law firms, audit firms, consulting practices, accounting and advisory firms seeking governance, compliance, and risk advisory.
  • Mining, Manufacturing & Industrial - Mining houses, manufacturers, processing plants, and supply chain operators needing operational compliance, risk mitigation, and contract governance.
  • Public Sector - Municipalities, government departments, state-owned enterprises (SOEs), and public agencies requiring statutory compliance and governance advisory.
  • Non-Profit & Social Impact Organisations - NGOs, NPOs, foundations, trusts, and donor-funded entities seeking governance frameworks, compliance support, and risk management solutions.
  • Logistics & Supply Chain - Compliance-driven logistics operators needing procurement, contract management, and operational governance support.
  • Healthcare - Medical practices and healthcare organisations requiring compliance frameworks, regulatory advisory, and risk management.
  • Construction & Infrastructure - Contractors and subcontractors requiring risk assessment, contract advisory, and compliance governance.
  • Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) - Businesses seeking turnkey compliance, governance, and operational management solutions to support sustainable growth.
