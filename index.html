ABOUT COMPANY

RMP Legal Compliance and Risk Advisory (Pty) Ltd is a South African firm providing legal compliance, risk management, labour and HR advisory, corporate governance, environmental, and SHEQ services.

The firm assists organisations in navigating complex regulatory requirements by offering strategic legal advice, compliance solutions, and risk mitigation frameworks aligned with governance, environmental responsibilities, and operational objectives.

Through a practical and collaborative approach, RMP works closely with clients to strengthen corporate governance, labour and HR compliance, environmental management, and operational risk systems. This enables organisations to operate ethically, manage regulatory obligations effectively, and achieve sustainable performance while maintaining compliance with national and international standards.
